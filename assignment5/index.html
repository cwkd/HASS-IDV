Suggested Improvements to Avocado Toast Visualisation

In assignment 2, one of the visualisations selected for critique was the apartment price visualisation (avocado toast visualisation) produced by the BBC. The content can be referenced below. We will be suggesting several improvements to the visualisation to allow it to fulfill its purpose.

Explanation

In the apartment price visualisation, the question (why/hypothesis) being asked is actually: how many years it would take to afford an apartment deposit in each of the 10 cities if one skipped breakfast. That being the case it makes more sense to compare it in terms of the cost of a typical breakfast in each city rather than the price of avocado toast which would depend partially on the cost of avocado in each city which is also influenced by other factors such as proximity of the city to the source of the avocado supply.

Since the issue is primarily with the idiom (how) rather than the data (what), an example bar chart has been done to show the number of years needed to afford an apartment deposit in each city. Click on the buttons to compare the deposit and breakfast prices between cities too.
